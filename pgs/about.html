Benjamin Conroy



Ben sees his work as a window into the zeitgeist, expressing and capturing the feelings of humanity through the bleeding of ink on paper. In his attempt to express his interpretation of humanity through an artistic lens, Ben has found his use of abstact drawing as a way to engage discussion in the hope of bringing progress.
